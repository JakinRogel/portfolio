Theology Studies

Studied Christian Biblical Studies at Grand Canyon University for 2 years.

I finished my studies with Global University, where I was credentialed as an Assemblies of God Minister.

Ministry

I served in my local church for two and a half years. My various roles were Children's Pastor, Young Adults Pastor, and responsibilities as required, which really just meant whatever else was needed at the time. I learned how to use different equipment, like a sound board, and how to run a conference and organize a team. Most of all I learned how to lead people in their faith while not letting my own faith slip away.

Where I Am Now

After serving in the church I wanted to take some time to try and develop myself even more. I felt like I became a pastor too soon. That I wasn't given the ability to develop the character necessary to succeed in the long run. I didn't feel like I was a man that was above reproach, as the Bible showed, and I knew I wasn't. So now I am trying to become that man. I am learning a skill, working hard with my hands, gaining the respect of outsiders, and loving all that I can while learning to be loved myself.

Future Ministry

The future is a mystery. But I know whatever the future might hold I will be pursuing Jesus with all my heart like I have been. I would love to plant a church someday, have it funded by donations with the tithe from the people going straight back to them to help bolster the community and their own lives. A place where fellowship is key, with Jesus and each other.
I plan on calling it, Christian Fellowship.
